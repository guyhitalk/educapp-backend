SCRIPTURE TOPICAL INDEX FOR STUDENT QUESTIONS

PURPOSE:
This index connects common student questions and topics to relevant Bible passages.
These verses help ground learning in God's Word and provide encouragement for students.

CREATION & SCIENCE:
Genesis 1:1 - "In the beginning, God created the heavens and the earth"
Genesis 1:27 - "God created man in his own image... male and female he created them"
Genesis 2:1-3 - God rested on the seventh day
Exodus 20:11 - Creation as basis for Sabbath pattern
Psalm 19:1 - "The heavens declare the glory of God"
Psalm 139:13-14 - "You formed my inward parts... I am fearfully and wonderfully made"
Romans 1:20 - Creation reveals God's invisible attributes
Colossians 1:16-17 - "By him all things were created... in him all things hold together"
Hebrews 11:3 - "By faith we understand that the universe was created by the word of God"

EDUCATION & LEARNING:
Deuteronomy 6:6-9 - Parents teach children God's Word diligently
Proverbs 1:7 - "The fear of the Lord is the beginning of knowledge"
Proverbs 2:6 - "The Lord gives wisdom; from his mouth come knowledge and understanding"
Proverbs 9:10 - "The fear of the Lord is the beginning of wisdom"
Proverbs 22:6 - "Train up a child in the way he should go"
Ecclesiastes 12:12 - Much study is weariness, but fear God and keep His commandments
2 Timothy 2:15 - "Do your best to present yourself to God as one approved"
2 Timothy 3:16-17 - Scripture equips for every good work
James 1:5 - "If any of you lacks wisdom, let him ask God"

DILIGENCE & HARD WORK:
Proverbs 10:4 - "A slack hand causes poverty, but the hand of the diligent makes rich"
Proverbs 12:24 - "The hand of the diligent will rule"
Proverbs 13:4 - "The soul of the diligent is richly supplied"
Proverbs 22:29 - "Do you see a man skillful in his work? He will stand before kings"
Ecclesiastes 9:10 - "Whatever your hand finds to do, do it with your might"
Colossians 3:23-24 - "Whatever you do, work heartily, as for the Lord and not for men"

TRUTH & INTEGRITY:
John 14:6 - "Jesus said... 'I am the way, and the truth, and the life'"
John 17:17 - "Sanctify them in the truth; your word is truth"
Psalm 119:105 - "Your word is a lamp to my feet and a light to my path"
Proverbs 12:22 - "Lying lips are an abomination to the Lord"
Ephesians 4:25 - "Having put away falsehood, let each one of you speak the truth"
Philippians 4:8 - "Whatever is true... think about these things"

OVERCOMING FRUSTRATION & FAILURE:
Psalm 34:18 - "The Lord is near to the brokenhearted"
Proverbs 24:16 - "The righteous falls seven times and rises again"
Romans 8:28 - "God works all things together for good for those who love him"
Philippians 4:13 - "I can do all things through him who strengthens me"
James 1:2-4 - "Count it all joy... when you meet trials of various kinds"
1 Peter 5:7 - "Cast all your anxieties on him, because he cares for you"

OBEDIENCE TO PARENTS:
Exodus 20:12 - "Honor your father and your mother"
Ephesians 6:1-3 - "Children, obey your parents in the Lord, for this is right"
Colossians 3:20 - "Children, obey your parents in everything"
Proverbs 1:8 - "Hear, my son, your father's instruction"
Proverbs 6:20 - "My son, keep your father's commandment"

PURPOSE & MEANING:
Ecclesiastes 12:13 - "Fear God and keep his commandments, for this is the whole duty of man"
Matthew 22:37-39 - "Love the Lord your God... Love your neighbor as yourself"
Romans 11:36 - "From him and through him and to him are all things"
1 Corinthians 10:31 - "Whether you eat or drink... do all to the glory of God"
Colossians 3:17 - "Whatever you do, in word or deed, do everything in the name of the Lord Jesus"

HUMAN NATURE & IDENTITY:
Genesis 1:27 - "God created man in his own image... male and female he created them"
Genesis 2:18-24 - God created marriage between man and woman
Psalm 8:4-6 - "What is man that you are mindful of him?... You made him a little lower than the heavenly beings"
Jeremiah 1:5 - "Before I formed you in the womb I knew you"

SALVATION & LIFE:
Genesis 2:7 - "The Lord God formed the man... and breathed into his nostrils the breath of life"
John 3:16 - "For God so loved the world, that he gave his only Son"
John 11:25 - "I am the resurrection and the life"
John 14:6 - "I am the way, and the truth, and the life. No one comes to the Father except through me"
Acts 4:12 - "There is salvation in no one else"
Romans 3:23 - "All have sinned and fall short of the glory of God"
Romans 6:23 - "The wages of sin is death, but the free gift of God is eternal life"
Romans 10:9 - "If you confess with your mouth the Lord Jesus and believe in your heart that God has raised Him from the dead, you will be saved"
Ephesians 2:8-9 - "By grace you have been saved through faith... not a result of works"

ENCOURAGEMENT IN STUDIES:
Proverbs 3:5-6 - "Trust in the Lord with all your heart... and he will make straight your paths"
Isaiah 40:31 - "They who wait for the Lord shall renew their strength"
Jeremiah 29:11 - "I know the plans I have for you, declares the Lord"
Matthew 11:28 - "Come to me, all who labor and are heavy laden, and I will give you rest"
2 Corinthians 12:9 - "My grace is sufficient for you, for my power is made perfect in weakness"
Galatians 6:9 - "Let us not grow weary of doing good"
Hebrews 12:11 - "All discipline seems painful... yet it yields the peaceful fruit of righteousness"

HOW TO USE THIS INDEX:
When students ask questions or face challenges, naturally reference relevant Scripture
to show them that God's Word speaks to all of life, including their studies. Help 
students see that learning is not separate from faith, but an expression of loving 
God with their minds.
